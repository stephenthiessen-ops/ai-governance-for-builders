# AI Model Approval Process

## Step 1 — Intake

- Business purpose defined
- Risk tier assigned
- Data sources identified

## Step 2 — Risk Review

Security + Governance review:
- Data exposure
- Regulatory impact
- Failure consequence

## Step 3 — Technical Review

- Prompt versioning
- Model selection
- Monitoring plan

## Step 4 — Approval Gate

Tier 1: Platform Lead  
Tier 2: Platform + Security  
Tier 3: Platform + Security + Legal + Exec Sponsor  

## Step 5 — Controlled Rollout

- Limited access
- Monitoring enabled
- Escalation path defined

Governance must be lightweight for low-risk systems and structured for high-risk systems.
